3. Unsupervised learning for finding outliers (anomaly)¶

       What would be an anomaly? Anomaly in our case would be any mismatch in the logic of the options. For example, the Bid (or Ask) prices of two call options that have the same Strike price but, say, 1-2 days difference in the Exercise day should be almost identical (unless there is something unusual, which should somehow be accounted for in the greeks). So, a big difference in the Bid prices of these two options would not be 'normal'. Or, for instance, high Theta or small Vega on an (OTM) option with long time to expiration. And so on.

For the purpose of anomaly detection we will use Isolation Forest.
